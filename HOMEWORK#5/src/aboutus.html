About us

Our company is the first in the region to organize this kind of comprtition. Our company exist in Macedonia since 2001. Folowing the trend in the modern way in kids education, we came to idea to organize this competition. The competition is intended for kids from 6 to 18 years old. The kids can compete individual or as a team.

The competition is organized in three categories, beginers (kids 6-10 years old), advansed (kids 11-14 years old) and coding-Lego category (kids 15-18 years old).

You can inform yourself on this webpage about the rules of comtetition and you can register your kid as individual competitor or you can register team of three kids who will compete together in the chalenge games.
